Oral presentations

All oral presenters are expected to submit their presentation slides (in pdf) through this link by EOD 9/6 Bulgaria time. The workshop organizers will help set up the presentations on the conference device. If any change is made after 9/6, presenters can bring their presentations on a USB stick in the coffee break 10 minutes before the session starts and to liaise with the session Chair and volunteers.

The length of the presentations is: 12 minutes presentation + 3 minutes questions. Please name your files according to the submisson number.

Poster presentations

All poster presenters are expected to upload the electronic version of their poster (in pdf) and a 3-5 minute pre-recorded presentation video through this link by 9/2. Please name your files according to the submisson number . The workshop organizers will then help put up all the pdfs on the workshop website. For the remote presentations, we will be using Zoom breakout rooms, which the attendees can move in and out of freely. Each presenter will be assigned a room, and will be allowed to share their poster or video with an audience. Questions can be typed in the chat for the presenters to answer. If you will attend in-person, bring your printed poster. The poster stands have the following size: A0, vertical (portrait orientation). Please make sure that you fix your poster before the poster session (12-1pm on Sept. 8). Poster presenters should stand next to their posters during most of the session. When the poster session finishes, presenters are asked to remove their posters.

Please make sure each paper has at least one registered author.

Please register for the workshop by using the RANLP registration form on this page: https://ranlp.org/ranlp2023/index.php/fees-registration/
